There is not know a lot about Neptune or its moons. Neptune was visited only by one spacecraft in 1989, and it managed to take a few pictures of its largest moon Triton.

As far as it is known, Neptune is a gas giant with not much to walk on and it isn’t possible to live on it.

Different story would be Triton, one of Neptune moons, that has a rocky surface covered with nitrogen ice. It has geysers that throw out liquid nitrogen and the slow atmospheric winds calmly drag it across all moon surface.

It is coldest moon in solar system averaging -235 degrees Celsius. It is believed that Triton wasn’t originally “created” in our solar system, but instead captured by Neptune’s gravity.

Triton is the only moon that orbits its planet on opposite direction than the orbit of Neptune. It has a thin atmosphere with gravity that’s only 8% from Earths. If you would want to walk across it in a straight line, it would take you 70 Earth days, but that would be quite a boring thing to do. And don’t think that you could pass time by texting your friends and family, as it would take 4 hours for your message to get to Earth, and 4 more hours to receive it.
