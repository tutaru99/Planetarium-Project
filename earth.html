According to radiometric dating Earth formed over 4.5 billion years ago. The only natural satellite of Earth is the moon.

Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System and also the largest of the five terrestrial planets. Approximately 71% of earth’s surface is covered in water.

Earth’s inner core is made out of solid iron core and it’s outer core is liquid iron which generates Earth’s magnetic field. The field’s primary role is to protect earth from the solar wind.

The current population of people on the earth is around - 7.7 billion.

Most of the places on the earth are survivable and habitable for people and animals alike. Earth ha only a handful places with severe temperature drops and hard conditions to survive, such as deserts and Russian taigas where temperature drops drastically. However, people overtime learned to adapt to harsh environments through variety of tool and housing insulation.

The biggest struggles on earth regarding surviving is hunger, war, plagues, illnesses and natural disasters as well as deteriorating condition of Earth’s resources which are vital to survival for people.
